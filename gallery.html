Khabib Submitting McGregor

This is Khabib's biggest victory. He won against the most popular athlete in the UFC, Conor McGregor. After a long rivalry, Khabib made McGregor submit due to a 4th round neck crank.
Khabib Submitting Poirier

After his victory over McGregor, Khabib beat top-contender Dustin Poirier, after submitting him in the third round due to a rear-naked choke.
