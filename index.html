It remains no surprise that Khabib is the most accomplished fighter in MMA history. Maintaining an undefeated record and dominating each of his opponents, he has reached a legendary status within the community of MMA. Khabib has never been hurt in any of his fights, because his fighting style has been perfected to every degree. He retired as the UFC Lightweight Champion, with a record of 29-0.
